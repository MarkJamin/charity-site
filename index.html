Professional Bio

Charity currently serves as a Leader of People Operations and Organizational Development, where she leads strategic initiatives that shape organizational culture, talent development, and workforce strategy. In this role, she works at the intersection of people and performance, designing systems and leadership programs that enable organizations to grow while empowering employees to succeed.

Her work focuses on modernizing performance management, strengthening leadership capability, and fostering workplace environments where employees can do their best work. Through her leadership, she helps organizations build sustainable talent pipelines and cultivate the next generation of leaders.

Charity’s leadership also extends to the broader professional community. As Director of Certifications for the Prince George’s County chapter of SHRM, she supports HR professionals seeking certification and career advancement. In this capacity, she plays a meaningful role in strengthening the HR profession and ensuring that organizations benefit from highly trained, ethical, and strategic HR leaders.

While her professional achievements are significant, Charity’s most visible impact can be seen through her entrepreneurial work in the community. She is the founder of P.S. Karma Arts and Crafts Enrichment Company, a youth enrichment organization dedicated to supporting the creative and emotional development of children.

Through after-school programs, camps, and creative workshops, P.S. Karma provides arts-based programming that fosters creativity, confidence, and social-emotional learning. The program is particularly focused on serving elementary-age children in high-risk communities, where access to enrichment opportunities can be limited.

Under Charity’s leadership, P.S. Karma has been recognized as the Best Arts and Crafts Program in the DMV region for five consecutive years, earning a place in the Hall of Fame. The organization has helped hundreds of children explore creativity, build confidence, and develop critical life skills through artistic expression.

Charity’s work demonstrates a powerful belief that creativity and education can transform lives. By creating spaces where children feel supported, encouraged, and inspired, she is helping to shape the next generation of confident and capable young leaders.

Across every area of her work, corporate leadership, professional service, and community entrepreneurship, Charity consistently demonstrates a commitment to empowering others and strengthening the communities she serves. Her ability to build systems, develop people, and create meaningful opportunities for growth reflects the very best qualities of a well rounded leader.

Through her professional leadership, dedication to service, and commitment to youth development, Charity Rankins continues to bring honor to the community through impact!
